Skyrim Troubleshooting

Sovngarde's timed crash

If you're playing a modded game of Skyrim and you've had no problem until getting to the end of the main questline. If you're pulling your hairs trying to fix the issue without ripping your load order apart and you just so happen to have installed the Royal Armory mod. You've found the fix here.

The most common reason for this freak glitch is a weird interaction with the mod Royal Armory and the "Season Unending" quest. This is the quest that plays out if you try to finish the main questline before winning the civil war. The leaders of the war go to High Hrothgar to make a truce so you can save the world. HOWEVER, having this mod installed turns this interaction into a timed bomb. From the moment you complete this quest the NPCs will start walking back home, but the very moment they get there... the game just breaks for some reason.

Now, to fix this problem... you can't. You'll just have to cut your loses, load an old save and finnish the civil war so you don't trigger the quest.
